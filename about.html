I graduated bachelor’s degree at Fashion Department at Academy of Fine Art in Warsaw in 2017 and then continue in master studies at Sculpture Department at the same Academy. Sculture Department helped me develop my skills in spatial design and also new media art such as movies, performance and photography.

In my designs I focus on mixing different colors, science-fiction and etno style. I like to use my own fabrics or recycled one. I designed costumes for many performances in Warsaw theatres. Since 2016 I am part of the independent theatre group „Potem-o-tem” based in Warsaw as a costume designer.
